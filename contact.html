Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our therapies, consultations, and your wellness journey.

For your first visit, please arrive 15 minutes early to complete intake forms. Bring any relevant medical records, a list of current medications, and wear comfortable clothing. Avoid heavy meals 2 hours before your appointment. Our practitioners will conduct a comprehensive assessment including pulse diagnosis, tongue examination, and detailed health history discussion.

Your initial consultation typically lasts 60-90 minutes. Our practitioner will assess your dosha constitution, discuss your health concerns, examine your pulse and tongue, and review your lifestyle and dietary habits. You'll receive a personalized treatment plan including dietary recommendations, herbal supplements, and suggested therapies. Follow-up consultations are usually 30-45 minutes.

Treatment duration varies based on your condition and goals. Acute issues may show improvement in 2-4 weeks, while chronic conditions typically require 3-6 months of consistent care. Panchakarma detox programs run 7-21 days. Individual therapy sessions range from 60-90 minutes. We emphasize sustainable lifestyle changes rather than quick fixes, ensuring lasting results and true healing.
